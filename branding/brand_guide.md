# Personal Brand Guide

This document outlines the core branding principles, design tokens, and style standards for Adith Shajee's professional portfolio and repositories.

---

## 1. Brand Identity

*   **Engineering Persona**: AI/ML Engineer focused on computer vision, generative AI frameworks, and scalable MLOps.
*   **Tone**: Technical, evidence-based, clean, structured, and professional.
*   **Key Message**: Bridging academic AI research and stable production-grade operations.

---

---

## 3. Typography

*   **Primary Typeface**: [Outfit](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Outfit) or [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) for clean headings and descriptive sections.
*   **Monospace Font**: [Fira Code](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Fira+Code) or [JetBrains Mono](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/JetBrains+Mono) for code blocks, badges, and terminal readouts.

---

## 4. Visual Hierarchy Rules

1.  **Header Styling**: Always prefix files with an H1 title showing the project name or persona.
2.  **Badges**: Restrict total badge count to 3-5 functional badges. Avoid excessive "language percentage" badges that clutter layout.
3.  **Tables**: Organize core skills and technologies inside neat markdown tables rather than long unstructured lists.
